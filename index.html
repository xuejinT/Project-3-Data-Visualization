Location and Number

There are a total of 126 restaurants around the world. Japan, being on top of the list, has a total of 31 3-star restaurants. France having 26 restaurants is ranked second place of having the most 3-star restaurants. United States is ranked third place with 14 restaurants across the state. This map below shows the countries with Michelin 3-star restaurants with the bubble indicating the number of restaurants.

Reservation or No

The dataset shows that there are 66 out of 126 (52.4%) restaurants requires reservation to dine in, while the other 60 do not. However, among the 60 restaurants, 19 restaurants advise their customers to make a reservation before going. That being said, there are still 41 (32.5%) restaurants who do not require reservation at all (it might be quite a wait).
